The Delft3D suite is composed of several modules, grouped around a mutual interface, while being capable to interact with one another. Delft3D-FLOW and Delft3D-WAVE (SWAN) are used in the present project. Complete documentations and users' manuals for the package can be found at the `Delft3D website <https://oss.deltares.nl/web/delft3d/manuals>`_.
